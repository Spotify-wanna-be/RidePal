About creators

By Admin

in Info

Meet our dynamic team comprising Martin, Dora, and Marian, united by a shared passion for transforming your travel experience. Together, we're on a mission to revolutionize how you enjoy your journeys. With our innovative application, we aim to redefine the soundtrack of your travels. By simply inputting your starting and ending points, our app curates personalized playlists tailored to the duration of your trip. Martin's expertise in coding, Dora's keen sense of user experience, and Marian's knack for music curation converge to create a seamless and enjoyable journey for every user. Join us on this exciting venture as we harmonize technology and travel, making your trips not only efficient but also musically memorable.
